Quick Facts About Me

  1. I'm twenty-three years old
  2. I am a student of Engineering Science at the University of Toronto
  3. I was born and raised in Vancouver, Canada
  4. I am an avid hiker/backpacker
  5. I'm a triathlete

Interests, Passions and Hobbies

  • Hiking: Walking through forests, camping on mountain peaks, and swimming in lakes — this sounds like the perfect adventure to me.
  • Triathlons: Since the age of 8 I've been competing in triathlons which are endurance races where you swim, bike and run.
  • Photography: This summer, I picked up a camera and I haven't been able to put it down. Capturing moments is a skill and an art, one that I would like to improve to better hold on to memories and share stories.
  • Geography: I know each country and capital of the world (on a good day).
  • Languages: I can speak advanced French and I want to start learning Spanish!
